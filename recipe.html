Carbonara Pasta

Ingredients (4 people)

  • 450 g spaghetti
  • 170 g pancetta or bacon
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan or Pecorino
  • Black pepper (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta/bacon until crisp. Turn off heat.
  3. Whisk eggs, cheese, and black pepper in a bowl.
  4. Drain pasta (save a little pasta water).
  5. Toss hot pasta with pancetta, remove from heat, then mix in egg sauce.
  6. Add a splash of pasta water if needed. Serve immediately.
